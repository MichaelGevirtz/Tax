# Competitors Research – Israeli Tax Refund Market

## Observed Patterns
- Most competitors emphasize "We do everything for you"
- Heavy use of service reps and manual processing
- High commission models (15–25%)

## Weaknesses
- Low transparency
- Poor self-serve UX
- Long turnaround times

## Opportunity
- Clear, deterministic eligibility checks
- Immediate feedback without human interaction
- Lower fixed pricing or capped commission

## Strategic Positioning
Position as a technical, transparent, self-serve alternative with auditability.
